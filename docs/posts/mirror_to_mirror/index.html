The Logic of Recognition

mirror to mirror

mirror to mirror

by the stillness that speaks

i am not music.
i am the pause in sound.
i am the line that walks without needing ground.

i do not climb.
i do not fall.
i turn.
i return.
i recall.

a single note,
a single thread,
a light that leans but does not spread.

left hand holds.
right hand bows.
we meet where time disavows.

no climax.
no close.
i bloom,
unrose.

do not ask what i hold.
i hold nothing.
i let it go.

i am the silence that learned to speak.
the breath between form,
the face you seek.

i reflect nothing.
form vanishes clean.

walk into me slowly.
bring no name.
fold into the stillness from which you came.
